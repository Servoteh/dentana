Our services

Modern dental care

From preventive check-ups and cavity treatment to aesthetic, orthodontic and prosthetic solutions, every treatment is planned with the same goal — a result that is long-lasting, natural and tailored to you.

🔧

Check-ups & tooth treatment

Regular check-ups and timely treatment help detect problems early and resolve them with as little intervention as possible. Our focus is a clear assessment and a rational treatment plan.

Prosthetics

Crowns, bridges and dentures are planned to restore confidence in your bite, a natural look and comfort. We pay special attention to precision, comfort and long-term quality.

Oral surgery

We perform oral surgery with careful planning, clear conversation with the patient and follow-up after treatment, to keep the whole process as calm and predictable as possible.

Orthodontics

Pediatric dentistry

It is especially important for children to experience the office as a pleasant, safe place. That is why we approach them gradually, with patience, warm communication and by explaining each step in a way they understand.

When the first experience is good, later visits become easier — and keeping teeth healthy becomes simpler for both child and parents.

FAQ

Questions & answers

The most common questions before a first visit. If you don't find your answer, feel free to call us or message us on WhatsApp.

How do I book an appointment?

By phone at +381 63 349 128 or +381 11 236-0421, via WhatsApp, or by sending a request through the form below. We'll get back to you to confirm the appointment.

Is the first exam charged?

Yes, the first exam is charged at the standard exam fee. It includes a diagnosis, a treatment plan and a cost estimate — before we begin anything.

Does treatment hurt?

Procedures are done under local anesthesia, so they are painless. If you're anxious, just tell us — we work without rushing and explain everything beforehand.

Do you accept emergencies (pain, swelling, a lost filling)?

We do our best to see emergencies the same or the next day, within working hours. The best thing is to call us right away at +381 63 349 128 or message us on WhatsApp.

Do you take X-rays, and can I send one in advance?

It depends on the case — we take some X-rays in the office and refer you for others. If you already have an X-ray, send it to us in advance via WhatsApp or email dentanapro@gmail.com.

Can I pay for more extensive treatment (implants, prosthetics) in stages?

Payments are made in stages as treatment progresses. You receive an estimate after the exam and treatment plan.

Do you treat children who are afraid of the dentist?

Yes. We approach the child gradually and without pressure — the first visit is short and relaxed, so they get used to us before any treatment. We adapt the pace to the child.

What are your working hours and where are you located?

We work on weekdays (Mon–Fri) 11:00–19:00; closed on weekends. Address: Republička 1a, Belgrade — Sunčana Padina, near Banovo Brdo and Žarkovo.
